It seems the semiconductor industry is driven purely by the top 0.001% of natural-born geniuses...
Not that other fields are different, but especially when you dive into this low-level design space, it's full of hidden masters.

These are completely unorganized, messy raw data, but I'm archiving them here just to show off my hard work.
